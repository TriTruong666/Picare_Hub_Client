## Design Context

### Users
Picare is used by end users accessing client applications and services. The interface should make available destinations immediately understandable and easy to enter.

### Brand Personality
Minimal, smooth, and professional. Interactions should feel controlled and refined, without decorative motion competing with content.

### Aesthetic Direction
A restrained dark interface with clear hierarchy and subtle, purposeful motion. Animation should clarify state changes and preserve a calm visual rhythm.

### Design Principles
- Keep primary actions and content immediately legible.
- Use motion sparingly, with smooth deceleration and visible sequencing.
- Avoid flashes, abrupt color changes, and competing hover effects.
- Preserve accessibility, including reduced-motion behavior.
